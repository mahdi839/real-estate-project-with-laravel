About My Sweet Home

We're more than just a real estate platform. We're your trusted partner in finding the perfect property that matches your dreams and budget.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, My Sweet Home has grown to become one of Bangladesh's most trusted real estate platforms. We started with a simple mission: to make property buying, selling, and renting transparent, efficient, and accessible to everyone.

Over the years, we've helped more than 50,000 clients find their dream properties across Dhaka and beyond. Our commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction has made us the go-to choice for real estate needs in Bangladesh.

Today, we continue to innovate and expand our services, bringing you the best properties, expert advice, and seamless experiences in the real estate market.

Our Mission

To provide transparent, reliable, and customer-centric real estate services that help people find their perfect homes and make informed property decisions.

Our Vision

To be Bangladesh's leading real estate platform, revolutionizing how people buy, sell, and rent properties through innovation and exceptional service.
